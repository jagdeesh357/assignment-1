Apple

An apple is a round fruit with red or green skin and a whitish inside. One variety of apple might be sweet, another sour.

Advantages of eating Apple:

  • Rich in dietary fiber, aiding digestion and promoting gut health.
  • Contains antioxidants that may help reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
  • Low in calories and high in vitamins, making it a healthy snack choice.

Orange

Oranges are a citrus fruit known for their tangy flavor and vibrant orange color.They are rich in vitamin C, making them a popular choice for boosting immune health.

Advantages of eating Oranges:

  • Rich source of vitamin C, which boosts immunity.
  • High in dietary fiber, promoting digestive health.
  • Contains antioxidants that may reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
